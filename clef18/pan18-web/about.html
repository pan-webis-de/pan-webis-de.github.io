Benefits for Early Birds

If you submit a working prototype of your software by March 1, 2018, we will pre-evaluate it on a subset of your task's test data and notify you of your performance. Based on the pre-evaluation, you may revise and resubmit your software for the full evaluation by April 15, 2018.
