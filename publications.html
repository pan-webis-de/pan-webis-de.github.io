Publications

All participants of PAN's shared tasks are invited to share their insights in the form of scientific papers. The papers comprise high-level descriptions of the digital text forensics technologies invented by participants to solve a given task. They are typically published in the context of conferences that host PAN's events, or as dedicated workshop proceedings.
