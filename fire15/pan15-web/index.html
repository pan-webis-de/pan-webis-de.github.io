AraPlagDet

Given a document written in Arabic, does it contain plagiarism?

This task offers to you the opportunity to evaluate your plagiarism detection method on Arabic documents. Is is composed of two sub-tasks: External Plagiarism Detection & Intrinsic Plagiarism Detection. Participants can participate to one or both sub-tasks.
